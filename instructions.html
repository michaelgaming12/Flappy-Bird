How to Play Flappy Bird

Welcome to Flappy Bird! Follow these instructions to play:

  • Start the Game: Press the Space key to start the game.
  • Keep the Bird Flying: Press the Space key to make the bird flap its wings and avoid hitting the pipes.
  • Avoid Pipes: The objective is to navigate the bird through the gaps between the pipes. If the bird hits a pipe or the ground, the game is over.
  • Score Points: Each time the bird passes through a pair of pipes without colliding, you earn a point.
  • Level Up: Every 10 points earned, you level up. You become faster and the pipes spawn further apart. Making it more difficult.
  • How to make it onto the leaderboard: You must earn a minimum of 10 points to make it onto the leaderboard.
  • Game Over: The game ends when the bird hits a pipe or the ground (or roof). Your final score will be displayed.
  • Restart: To play again, press the Space key after the game ends.
